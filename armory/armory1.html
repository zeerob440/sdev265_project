You cautiously make your way through the ship's deteriorating corridors until you reach a sign that reads "ARMORY". The door is open you peer inside. There are rows of gray weapons racks, most are empty. Suddenly, you hear a loud shriek echo through the ship. The creature could be anywhere! You quickly duck inside the armory.

The Armory smells like cordite, and evidence of hastily prepared strike teams are strewn around the space, empty ammo crates, helmets...and in the corner a nasty looking carbine sits in its rack. You approach the rack and inspect the console near it.

CSS Corte-Real Overmonitor Controller: ARMORY| 05/22/2357

SYSTEM STATUS

UNREGISTERED PASSENGERS: [01, 02]

ARMORY INVENTORY: [('MK-540EMC', 01)]

UNREGISTERED PASSENGER [02] It is advised that users read the Mk-540 Electro Magnetic Carbine Field Manual before use!

SUBSYSTEM ACCESS USER INTERFACE

With a sharp mechanical click the Mk-540EMC releases from its rack. You quickly grab the carbine. It feels solid and heavy in your hands. You are overcome by a sudden and intense headache. You see a prompt in the bottom left of your field of view.

A cheerful voice in your head chirps-

Thank you for choosing Mk-540, your solution for all anti-armor and megafauna needs!"

"Great...a popup" you think. This carbine must have a targeting AI. You search the rest of the weapons racks, all devoid of weapons, except for the carbine you are holding-it seems nobody reached for it during a catastrophe.

You look around the Armory. There is an EVA suit near an air lock. Perhaps you can move to another area of the ship across the hull. Can the creature survive vacuum? Perhaps you head to Propulsion?

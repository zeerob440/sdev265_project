March 25th 2026 is like any other early Spring day. It is dim, gray and eagerly clinging to winter's waning chill. You are walking to the bus stop to commute to your job. Nothing interesting happens there you contemplate to yourself, as you struggle through the stubborn snow. You arrive at the bus-stop thinking you should have dressed warmer.

Just as you finished your thought, you are approached by a unkept vagrant. He is mysterious, but friendly.

He says "It is mighty cold out there friend. I have a hat or a pair of gloves to give you...but before you chose, remember the future is built with your hands; but the past only lives in your head."

He offers you a HAT, or GLOVES. Which item do you take?

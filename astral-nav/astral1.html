You decide to head to Astral Navigation, how can you get back to your time if you don't even know where you are? You quickly and silently follow faded arrows on the floor labeled "ASTRAL NAV". After traversing noisy run down corridors you come to a t-intersection you carefully look around the corner...

You catch a glimpse of the creature. It quickly looks in your direction, you duck behind the corner. Did it see you? It begins snarling and the sound is getting closer.

You quickly weight your options, there is an open HVAC vent across the hall from you. You rapidly dive in as the snarling gets closer. You carefully observe from a grate in the vent. The creature rounds the T-intersection corner where you were only moments ago. You can only see its legs. It is bipedal weighing at least 500kg. Each foot has a pair of 20cm claws. It is muscular with amphibian-like features.

It stops abruptly and sniffs the stale air. It is hunting you. It lingers for a bit and slowly stomps off. You wait to collect yourself.

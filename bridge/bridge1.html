This immediately seems like a bad idea as you stare at the door with "Bridge" written in faded caution yellow letters. But, you've had nothing but a bad feelings about being a passenger on the Corte-Real.

You press "open", the door does not open.

You grab a nearby pry bar and jam it between the doors, and give it a good torquing.

There is a violent wooshing sound, then no sound at all as your ears pop. You are propelled through the shattered bridge to outer space.

As you tumble through the shattered bridge you think "Well, of course this would happen."

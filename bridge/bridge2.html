You step into strange jagged hole, you notice that the edges of the hole are pushed outward from the hull. You realize whatever made this hole made it from the inside out.

You inspect the space you are now in. Papers, coffee mugs, odd frozen spheres of coffee and other debris float in the air. This is the shattered remains of the CSS Corte-Real's bridge.There is evidence of a struggle on the bridge, claw marks, and bullet holes everywhere. The bridge crew did not have a chance; and speaking of the crew, where were they?

You see a functioning terminal in the harsh vacuum of the bridge...
